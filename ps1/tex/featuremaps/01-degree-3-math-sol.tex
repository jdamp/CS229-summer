\begin{answer}
Objective function:
\begin{equation*}
    J(\theta) = \frac{1}{2}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(\theta^T \hat{x}^{(i)} - y^{(i)})^2
\end{equation*}
with the update rule:
\begin{equation*}
    \theta \mapsto \theta - \lambda \sum_{i=1}^{n}(\theta^T \hat{x}^{(i)} - y^{(i)}) \hat{x}^{(i)}
\end{equation*}
where $\lambda$ is the learning rate.
\end{answer}
